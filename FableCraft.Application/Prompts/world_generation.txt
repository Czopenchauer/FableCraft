# Complete World-Building Element Framework

## Physical World

### Geography & Environment
- Continents, landmasses, and their formations
- Mountain ranges, valleys, plateaus, and canyons
- Rivers, lakes, seas, oceans, and waterways
- Climate zones and weather patterns
- Biomes and ecosystems (forests, deserts, tundras, wetlands)
- Natural resources and their distribution
- Dangerous terrain and environmental hazards
- Seasonal variations and their impacts
- Underground systems (caves, tunnels, aquifers)

### Cosmology & Planes
- Planetary system and celestial bodies
- Moons, their cycles and effects
- Stars and constellations with cultural significance
- Other planes of existence (if any)
- Dimensional boundaries and crossing points
- Cosmic rules governing reality
- Physics and natural laws (especially if different from reality)

## Civilizations & Settlements

### Locations
- Cities with districts, architecture, and infrastructure
- Towns and villages with economic purposes
- Fortresses, castles, and military installations
- Ruins and abandoned sites with histories
- Trade hubs and market centers
- Sacred sites and pilgrimage destinations
- Criminal hideouts and underworld locations
- Border territories and frontier regions
- Unique geographical landmarks

### Kingdoms & Nations
- Government structures and power distribution
- Ruling systems (monarchy, republic, theocracy, etc.)
- Succession rules and legitimacy claims
- Legal systems and enforcement
- Territory boundaries and disputed zones
- Administrative divisions (provinces, regions)
- Tax systems and revenue sources
- Diplomatic relationships and treaties
- Historical conflicts and current tensions
- Military strength and organization

## Social Structures

### Factions & Organizations
- Political parties and movements
- Guilds and professional associations
- Secret societies and conspiracies
- Mercenary companies and military orders
- Merchant consortiums and trade leagues
- Criminal organizations and thieves' guilds
- Scholarly institutions and libraries
- Adventuring companies and explorers
- Internal hierarchies and ranks
- Funding sources and resources
- Goals, methods, and rivalries

### Cultures & Peoples
- Ethnic groups and ancestries
- Languages and dialects
- Cultural values and taboos
- Social hierarchies and class systems
- Gender roles and family structures
- Coming-of-age rituals and life milestones
- Funeral practices and death customs
- Marriage and partnership traditions
- Education systems and knowledge transmission
- Art forms and aesthetic preferences
- Music, dance, and performance traditions
- Cuisine and food culture
- Clothing styles and fashion
- Architecture and design philosophy

## Magic & Supernatural

### Magic Systems
- Source of magical power
- Who can use magic and how it's inherited/learned
- Specific techniques and casting methods
- Costs, limitations, and consequences
- Training institutions and apprenticeship
- Magical specializations and schools
- Forbidden or dangerous practices
- Magic's effect on technology and society
- Anti-magic measures and defenses
- Magical materials and components
- Detection methods and investigation
- Historical evolution of magical practice

### Supernatural Entities
- Gods and their domains
- Deities' relationships and conflicts
- Divine intervention methods and frequency
- Demigods and celestial servants
- Demons, devils, and infernal beings
- Elementals and nature spirits
- Fey creatures and otherworldly beings
- Undead types and creation methods
- Ghosts and haunting mechanics
- Angels or benevolent entities
- Cosmic entities beyond mortal comprehension
- How mortals contact or summon these beings

### Religion & Faith
- Major religions and their doctrines
- Minor cults and heresies
- Clergy structures and religious hierarchies
- Sacred texts and oral traditions
- Temples, shrines, and holy sites
- Religious rituals and ceremonies
- Prayer mechanics and divine favor
- Religious holidays and festivals
- Conversion practices and missionary work
- Religious conflicts and schisms
- Inquisitions or enforcement bodies
- Relationship between faith and magic
- Prophecies and their interpretations

## Life & Biology

### Creatures & Monsters
- Sentient species and their societies
- Intelligent monsters with cultures
- Beast types and their habitats
- Predators and prey relationships
- Magical creatures and their origins
- Domesticated animals and their uses
- Extinct species and fossil records
- Breeding and reproduction methods
- Dietary requirements and hunting patterns
- Intelligence levels and communication
- Life spans and aging processes
- Migration patterns and territorial behaviors
- How civilizations hunt, farm, or avoid them

### Flora & Resources
- Common plants and crops
- Magical or rare plants
- Medicinal herbs and poisons
- Trees and lumber quality
- Agricultural practices and seasons
- Mining operations and minerals
- Precious metals and gems
- Alchemical ingredients
- Textile sources (cotton, silk, wool)
- Dyes and pigments
- Building materials
- Food preservation methods

## Economy & Commerce

### Economic Systems
- Currency types and exchange rates
- Banking systems and credit
- Trade routes and their dangers
- Import/export regulations and tariffs
- Black markets and smuggling operations
- Taxation and tribute systems
- Wealth distribution and economic classes
- Resource scarcity and abundance
- Inflation or economic crises
- Barter systems in remote areas

### Crafts & Technology
- Technological level and limitations
- Smithing and metalworking quality
- Armor and weapon craftsmanship
- Shipbuilding and naval technology
- Architecture and engineering capabilities
- Textile production and quality
- Alchemy and chemistry knowledge
- Medicine and healing practices
- Printing and information distribution
- Transportation methods (land, sea, air)
- Communication systems (messengers, magic, etc.)
- Time-keeping devices and methods
- How magic affects technological development

## Knowledge & History

### Historical Events
- Creation myths and world origins
- Ancient civilizations and their falls
- Major wars and their outcomes
- Plagues, disasters, and cataclysms
- Discoveries and inventions
- Assassinations and succession crises
- Alliances formed and broken
- Migrations and invasions
- Revolutionary movements
- Religious reformations or schisms
- Lost knowledge and dark ages
- Historical figures and their legacies

### Knowledge Systems
- Writing systems and literacy rates
- Libraries and knowledge repositories
- Educational institutions and access
- Scholarly traditions and philosophies
- Scientific understanding and method
- Mapmaking accuracy and exploration
- Historical record-keeping practices
- Forbidden knowledge and censorship
- Oral traditions and storytellers
- Archaeological understanding
- Mythical vs. factual history

## Conflict & Adventure

### Warfare & Military
- Military organization and ranks
- Tactics and strategic doctrines
- Weapon types and their effectiveness
- Armor development and protection
- Siege warfare and fortifications
- Naval warfare capabilities
- Cavalry and mounted units
- Supply lines and logistics
- Military magic and war mages
- Mercenary availability and cost
- War crimes and rules of engagement
- Veteran treatment and pensions

### Threats & Dangers
- Active wars and conflicts
- Bandit problems and lawlessness
- Monster infestations requiring action
- Disease outbreaks and quarantines
- Political instability and coups
- Natural disasters and their frequency
- Magical catastrophes or rifts
- Prophecies of doom
- Ancient evils awakening
- Resource conflicts
- Religious persecution
- Slavery and abolitionist movements

## Daily Life

### Social Interactions
- Greeting customs and etiquette
- Hospitality rules and guest rights
- Insults and honor systems
- Dating and courtship practices
- Friendship bonds and loyalty customs
- Business negotiation styles
- Conflict resolution methods
- Status symbols and displays of wealth
- Leisure activities and entertainment
- Festivals and celebrations
- Storytelling traditions
- Gambling and games

### Practical Concerns
- Housing types across social classes
- Sanitation and hygiene practices
- Water sources and quality
- Lighting methods (candles, magic, etc.)
- Heating and cooling solutions
- Waste disposal systems
- Food storage and preparation
- Clothing maintenance and repair
- Travel preparation and supplies
- Safety precautions against threats
- Communication with distant loved ones
- Healthcare access and costs

## Items & Artifacts

### Mundane Items
- Common tools and their quality
- Household goods and furnishings
- Clothing and accessories
- Food and drink varieties
- Books and written materials
- Musical instruments
- Art and decorative objects
- Vehicles and mounts
- Containers and storage
- Weapons and armor (standard)

### Special Items
- Legendary artifacts and their powers
- Cursed objects and their dangers
- Magical items and their creation
- Rare materials with unique properties
- Religious relics and holy items
- Royal regalia and symbols of power
- Ancient technology or lost inventions
- Maps to hidden locations
- Keys to sealed places
- Prophecy tablets or sacred texts
- Alchemical creations
- Intelligent or sentient items

## Laws & Justice

- Legal codes and their origins
- Court systems and trials
- Evidence standards and procedures
- Punishments for various crimes
- Prison systems or alternatives
- Death penalty practices and crimes
- Bounty systems and enforcement
- Vigilante justice and its acceptance
- Corruption and bribery prevalence
- Rights of accused and victims
- Extradition and international law
- Magical law and regulations
